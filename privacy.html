Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

1 Introduction

ReVision ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user data in the ReVision macOS application.

2 Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information, documents, images, or usage data.

Your data stays on your Mac. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

3 Local Processing

All operations performed by ReVision — including PDF conversion, OCR text recognition, AI-powered background inpainting, and image processing — are executed entirely on your device. Your files never leave your computer and are never uploaded to any cloud server or third-party service.

4 AI Models

ReVision uses on-device AI models (including LaMa for background inpainting and U²-Net for image segmentation). These models run locally on your Mac and do not require an internet connection. No data is sent to external servers for AI processing.

5 Third-Party Services

The Application operates entirely offline for its core functionality. We do not use third-party analytics, tracking tools, or cloud-based APIs.

6 Camera Access

ReVision may request camera access solely for capturing images to be converted into presentations. Camera data is processed locally and is never transmitted externally.

7 File Access

ReVision requests access to your files (Desktop, Documents, Downloads) solely to read the PDF and image files you choose to open. We do not scan, index, or access any files beyond those you explicitly select.

8 Contact Us
