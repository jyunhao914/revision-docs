How It Works

Three Steps to Your First Recording

1

Import Your PDF Slides

Tap "Open PDF" on the home screen and pick your presentation from Files, iCloud Drive, or drop it in via AirDrop. SlideCast renders every page and shows a progress indicator for longer decks.

2

Configure Camera, Audio & Layout

Choose front or rear camera, pick your microphone, and set the instructor PiP size and position. Select landscape or portrait output. Preview the final composition in real time before you start.

3

Record → Edit → Export

Tap record. Swipe or use your clicker to advance slides. When done, trim or re-record any segment in the timeline editor. Export with one tap — the video lands in your Photos library.

🔒

100% On-Device. Zero Uploads.

SlideCast never connects to the internet. Your slides and recordings stay on your device — always. No accounts, no trackers, no ads.

📖 FAQ

Q: What PDF formats are supported?
All standard PDFs work, including those with embedded images and vector graphics.

Q: Can I use the rear camera?
Yes — switch between front and rear camera in the recording settings before you start.

Q: Does SlideCast upload my videos?
No. Everything is processed and stored on your device. SlideCast has no internet connection.

Q: Is the iPad version identical to iPhone?
Yes, SlideCast is a universal app with the same feature set on both iPhone and iPad.
